## ðŸŽ¯ **Campaign Optimization Strategies**

### Creative Optimization
1. **High-Performing Elements**:
   - Professional imagery with real people
   - Clear value propositions with numbers/metrics
   - Company logos for social proof
   - Clean, modern design aesthetic

2. **Message Optimization**:
   - Lead with specific pain points
   - Include quantified benefits (time/money saved)
   - Use industry-specific language
   - Strong, action-oriented CTAs

3. **Video Best Practices**:
   - Hook within first 3 seconds
   - Show actual product interface
   - Include customer testimonials
   - Clear audio for mobile viewing

### Audience Optimization
1. **Expansion Strategy**:
   - Start narrow, expand based on performance
   - Use similar audiences for scaling
   - Test adjacent job titles and industries
   - Leverage account-based targeting

2. **Exclusion Strategy**:
   - Exclude converted customers
   - Remove low-performing demographics
   - Filter out irrelevant job titles
   - Exclude competing companies' employees

### Bidding Optimization
1. **Enhanced CPC**:
   - Start with enhanced CPC for control
   - Move to automated bidding after 50+ conversions
   - Use target CPA when sufficient data available

2. **Budget Management**:
   - Allocate more budget to winning audiences
   - Pause underperforming ad sets
   - Increase bids for high-value targets
