## üé® **Creative Testing Framework**

### Testing Philosophy
- **Statistical Significance**: Minimum 100 conversions per variation
- **Testing Duration**: 1-2 weeks per test cycle
- **Variation Limit**: Maximum 3 variations per test to maintain statistical power
- **Winner Threshold**: >20% improvement to declare statistical winner

### Creative Elements to Test
1. **Headlines**: Problem vs Solution vs Social Proof
2. **Value Propositions**: Price vs Time vs Integration
3. **CTAs**: Action vs Benefit vs Urgency
4. **Visual Elements**: Screenshots vs People vs Graphics
5. **Message Tone**: Professional vs Conversational vs Technical
